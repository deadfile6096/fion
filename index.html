Galactic FION Hub Whitepaper

Welcome to the Frontier!

Galactic FION Hub is an economic simulation where you manage your own trading planet in a distant galaxy.

How to Play

  • Create your character to begin your journey
  • Hire alien traders from the Planetary Customs
  • Build structures to improve your planet's capabilities
  • Create and trade goods to earn $FION
  • Climb the Capitalism Board to become the greatest trader in the galaxy

Game Elements

Traders

Each trader has unique skills: Trading, Persuasion, Delivery Speed, and more. Rare traders (with glowing backgrounds) produce twice the $FION!

Buildings

Buildings provide various bonuses:

  • Spaceport: Increases trader arrival frequency
  • Alien Hotel: Improves chance of rare traders
  • Gravimolecule Factory: Speeds up goods production
  • Magnetic Market: Increases demand for goods

Goods

Goods are produced by your traders over time. Each good has a production level that determines how quickly it's created.

Planet Levels

Your planet levels up as you achieve milestones:

  • Level 2: Hire 5 traders and build 2 structures
  • Level 3: Hire 10 traders and build 5 structures
  • Level 4: Hire 20 traders and build 10 structures
  • Level 5: Hire 30 traders and build 15 structures

Remember: The galaxy is vast and full of opportunity. Trade wisely!
